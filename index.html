Travel to Mexico

Mexico is a country located in the southern part of North America. It is known worldwide for its cultural richness, its gastronomy and its diversity in natural resources.

Places to visit

There are some famous places to visit like:

  1. Chichen Itza, near Cozumel.
  2. Temple of Warriors, near Cozumel .
  3. Laguna Bacalar, near Costa Maya.
  4. Todos Santos Island, Cabo San Lucas.
